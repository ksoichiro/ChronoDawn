In the desert biome stands a tower of gears and clockwork.

Inside, you'll find Enhanced Clockstone - a material for advanced tools and weapons.

The tower is guarded by the [Time Guardian](^chronodawn:bosses/time_guardian), a mini-boss that must be defeated to progress further.
