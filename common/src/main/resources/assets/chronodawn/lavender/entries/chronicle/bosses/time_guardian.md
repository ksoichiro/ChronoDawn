This mini-boss guards the path to the Master Clock.

Defeat it to obtain the Key to Master Clock.

**Strategy:**
Use Time Clock to cancel its attacks. The Time Guardian can launch ranged Time Blast projectiles that apply debuffs, so maintain distance when possible.
