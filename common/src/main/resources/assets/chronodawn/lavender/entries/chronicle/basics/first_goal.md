Seek the [Forgotten Library](^chronodawn:structures/forgotten_library), a structure of knowledge lost to time.

Within its walls, you will find the recipe for the [Portal Stabilizer](^chronodawn:progression/portal_stabilizer) - your key to returning home.

Without it, your portal remains one-way only.
