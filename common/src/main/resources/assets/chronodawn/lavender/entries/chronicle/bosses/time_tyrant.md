The final guardian of the Stasis Core.

**Strategies:**
- Time Arrows inflict severe debuffs
- Destroy Temporal Anchors to weaken abilities
- Bring backup supplies

The Time Tyrant has multiple phases with different attack patterns. Be prepared for a long battle.
