These mechanical guardians roam the Chrono Dawn, immune to Time Distortion effects.

Defeating them drops Ancient Gears, which are required to access the Time Tyrant's chamber.

You need 3 Ancient Gears to unlock the boss room door in the Master Clock Tower.
