# Custom Mob Ideas for Chrono Dawn

## Hostile Mobs

### 1. Epoch Husk

* **Vanilla Equivalent**: Zombie
* **HP**: 24 (♥×12)
* **Traits**:
  * Deploys a short-duration "time slowdown aura" when damaged
  * Slightly reduces player's attack speed
* **Appearance**:
  * Weathered humanoid form with parts crumbling like an hourglass
  * Dimly glowing clock hands visible inside the body

→ The most natural replacement for zombies that fits the mod's world.

---

### 2. Secondhand Archer

* **Vanilla Equivalent**: Skeleton
* **HP**: 20
* **Traits**:
  * Arrows have "delayed impact", landing slightly late then accelerating
  * Movement periodically shifts in a teleport-like manner
* **Appearance**:
  * Elongated skeletal frame with arms as long as clock second hands
  * Gear-shaped bow

---

### 3. Paradox Crawler

* **Vanilla Equivalent**: Spider
* **HP**: 18
* **Traits**:
  * Leaves "afterimage trails" when moving on walls/ceilings
  * Attacks inflict a short-duration vision blur debuff
* **Appearance**:
  * Legs temporally disconnected in sections
  * Afterimages follow with a delay as it moves

---

### 4. Moment Creeper

* **Vanilla Equivalent**: Creeper
* **HP**: 22
* **Traits**:
  * Momentarily "completely freezes" before exploding
  * Explosion causes minimal terrain damage but strong time debuff
* **Appearance**:
  * Glass-like body surface with frozen explosion effect inside

---

### 5. Timeline Strider

* **Vanilla Equivalent**: Enderman
* **HP**: 32
* **Traits**:
  * Teleportation is linear with visible "trajectory"
  * Returns to past position when attacked
* **Appearance**:
  * Slender form with glowing trajectory lines on back
  * Timeline afterimages appear in sequence during teleport

※ Differentiated from Clockwork Sentinel by focusing on "timeline" rather than "machinery".

---

### 6. Hourglass Golem

* **Vanilla Equivalent**: Iron Golem
* **HP**: 60
* **Traits**:
  * Attack speed increases as HP decreases
  * Creates time slowdown field at feet
* **Appearance**:
  * Torso is a giant hourglass
  * Behavior changes as sand falls

---

### 7. Forgotten Minute

* **Vanilla Equivalent**: Vex
* **HP**: 14
* **Traits**:
  * Appears suddenly and disappears after a short time
  * Strengthens nearby enemies when damaged
* **Appearance**:
  * Semi-transparent with blurred outlines
  * Constantly fading in and out

---

### 8. Chronal Leech

* **Vanilla Equivalent**: Silverfish
* **HP**: 10
* **Traits**:
  * Attacks extend player's cooldowns
  * Spawns in groups
* **Appearance**:
  * Elongated form with number-like patterns on surface

---
